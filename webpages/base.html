About

Welcome to tex - where texchanging books is made easy!

Created as a project for COS 333, tex simplifies buying and selling textbooks. Easily compare prices from on- and off-campus offers so that you can get the best price for your books.

Looking to buy a book? With just one search, you can check out offers from on- and off-campus (Amazon, Labyrinth, Chegg, and Campus).

Or hoping to get rid of your old textbooks? Just find your book, and set up an offer or start an auction to get the best price.

What if the book you want isn't available? You can follow the book that you want and we'll email you the moment someone offers it.


Created by: Ameera Abdelaziz '15, Jeffrey Asala '15, Nicole Loncke '15, and Laura Xu '15
