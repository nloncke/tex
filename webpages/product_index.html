What is tex?
tex is a site for buying and selling books on campus. Learn all about our fantextic features here!


Buying made easy!

  • Search by title, course, author, or ISBN for fast and accurate results.


  • See the prices of all auctions and offers in the same place.


  • You choose your price! With our auction feature, you can now bid on books sold by fellow students. The time remaining in the auction is always displayed as a countdown so there are no surprises. If you are in a hurry to get the book, you can pay the Buy Now price and immediately win the auction!

  • Can't find the book you seek? Search by ISBN to instantly add the book to our database. It's as easy as that!

Selling made easy!

  • If you're looking to sell you can search for your book with the search bar. Alternatively, you can use our quick sell feature and be taken directly to the sell form.


  • Use other campus offers, the Amazon list price, and links to other sites to help you gauge your selling point!


  • With our auction feature, students can now bid on the books you want to sell! You control the baseline price for the book and when the auction will end. You can also set a maximum price at which you'd be happy to sell the book immediately.

Account made easy!

  • Stay in the loop: follow a book that isn't currently offered or is too expensive right now. You will be alerted when a new offer is made! Once you find a satisfactory offer, you can unfollow to quit receiving notifications.


  • Activity Notifications: When events directly concerning you occur, you will receive an email with the important details.

  • Follow a book that isn't currently offered or is too expensive right now.

Miscellaneous

  • Stay in the loop: receive real-time notifications
  • Add a new book to the site simply by searching by ISBN! (ISBN 10 and 13 supported)
